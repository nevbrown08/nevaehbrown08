About

Our Mission

We build a friendly, creative community for fans to share art, stories, and resources.

Team & Volunteers

Small volunteer-run group with rotating roles:

  • Coordinator — organizes events and meetups
  • Archivist — maintains episode lists and resources
  • Gallery curator — reviews submitted artwork

Community Guidelines

  1. Be respectful and kind.
  2. Give proper credit for artwork and writing.
  3. Do not post copyrighted material without permission.

Accessibility

This fan site aims to meet WCAG 2.1 AA where possible. Features implemented include skip link, semantic headings, form labels, alt text examples, visible focus, and keyboard-friendly controls.

Known limitations

  • Some images are external placeholders — ensure public direct links or host locally before publishing.
  • Add transcripts/captions for any multimedia prior to release.
